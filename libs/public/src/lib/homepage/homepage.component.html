L`Chaim

About L'chaim:L'Chaim was established as an NGO September 20, 2022. The Association is a voluntary, non-governmental, non-profit Association located in the Czech Republic focused on the support and empowerment of those engaged in the sex industry, both voluntarily and involuntarily. This includes education, awareness, and field work pertaining to human trafficking and exploitation of sex industry workers.

Volunteers
