L`Chaim

Our Mission: Help us promote shalom (peace) and empower women in prostitution. Our holistic care and services include crisis hotline, social integration courses, brothel visits, language lessons, medical help, and counseling. Join us in supporting these women to live a healthy and fulfilling life. About L'chaim: We started as a vision of building friendships with women in prostitution in Prague. Now, we are a trained and equipped team expanding to Brno. Join us in our journey to make a difference in the lives of women in prostitution.
