Help Support Our Mission to Empower Women in Prostitution

We believe that every woman deserves the opportunity to live a life that is healthy for their soul and body, and we work tirelessly to provide the support they need to overcome the challenges they face.

Our organization relies on the generosity of donors like you to continue our important work. Your contribution will go directly towards providing crisis hotline services, social integration courses, brothel visits, English and Czech language lessons, medical help, and counseling services to women in prostitution. By donating to L'chaim, you will be directly impacting the lives of these women and helping them to build a brighter future.
