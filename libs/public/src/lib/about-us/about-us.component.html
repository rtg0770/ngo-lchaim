L`Chaim

The word L’Chaim is a Hebrew word which means “to life” and the vision of L’Chaim Czech Republic is to see women in prostitution LIVE LIFE in a way that is healthy to their soul and bodies, and which promotes shalom (peace). This is achieved through providing holistic care and services, both by L’Chaim team members and by various partner organisations.

Our Mission

 “At L'chaim, our mission is to provide holistic care and services to women in prostitution, with the goal of promoting shalom (peace) and helping them live a healthy and fulfilling life. We offer crisis hotline services, social integration courses, brothel visits, English and Czech language lessons, medical help, and counseling services. Our team is dedicated to empowering women and providing them with the support they need to overcome the challenges they face. Our vision is to see women in prostitution thriving and living a life that is healthy for their soul and body.".

About L`chaim

 In 2011 Yael Schoultz started visiting women in prostitution in the brothels of Prague, Czech Republic, building friendships, and developing the vision to see a team trained and equipped to provide care and services to women in prostitution. Slowly this team formed, and in 2014 a two year certificate training programme was established to further educate and equip team members to be more effective in their care for women in prostitution. In 2018 the service to women in prostitution was expanded to include not only Prague but also the second largest city of the Czech Republic which is named Brno.
