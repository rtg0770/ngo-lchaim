About

L'Chaim was established as an NGO September 20, 2022. The Association is a voluntary, non-governmental, non-profit Association located in the Czech Republic focused on the support and empowerment of those engaged in the sex industry, both voluntarily and involuntarily. This includes education, awareness, and field work pertaining to human trafficking and exploitation of sex industry workers.

Goals of the Association

 The main goals of the Association are:
a) provide skills training to sex industry workers to empower them for broader job opportunities;
b) be a bridge between sex industry workers and organisations that can aid in their empowerment;
c) be a bridge between sex industry workers and state authorities, including law enforcement bodies;
d) fight against social discrimination and marginalisation which contributes to the disempowerment of those working in sex industry, for example, human trafficking;
e) provide skills training to sex workers to enable them to access wider employment opportunities;
f) prevent and intervene in human trafficking through school education programmes, for example, among the Roma/Sinti and other marginalised and at risk populations.
